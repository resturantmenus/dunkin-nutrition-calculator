Smart Swaps to Cut Calories

Here are some easy changes that make a big difference:

  • Choose almond milk instead of whole milk. This saves about 60 calories per drink.
  • Skip the flavor swirls. Each pump adds around 50 calories. Ask for sugar free options instead.
  • Say no to whipped cream. You will save 80 calories instantly.
  • Go for a smaller size. A small latte has nearly half the calories of a large one.
  • Order iced drinks. They often have slightly fewer calories than frozen versions because frozen drinks contain more sugar.

Tips for Ordering Healthy at Dunkin

After helping thousands of readers track their Dunkin orders, I have learned a few tricks that actually work:

  • Plan your order before you go. Use the calculator at home so you know exactly what to ask for.
  • Do not be afraid to customize. Dunkin staff are happy to make changes. Ask for light cream, less sugar, or sugar free syrups.
  • Pair your coffee with protein. If you need food, go for the Wake Up Wrap. It has only 180 calories but gives you 9 grams of protein to keep you full.
  • Watch the seasonal drinks. Pumpkin Spice and holiday drinks often have double the calories of regular menu items.
  • Stay consistent. One high calorie treat will not ruin your diet. But daily 500 calorie lattes add up quickly over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many calories are in a medium Dunkin iced coffee?

A medium iced coffee with no cream or sugar has only 5 calories. Adding cream and sugar can bring it up to 100 calories or more.

What is the lowest calorie drink at Dunkin?

Black coffee and unsweetened iced tea have the lowest calories at just 5 calories per serving.

Does Dunkin have sugar free options?

Yes. Dunkin offers sugar free flavor shots in vanilla, hazelnut, toasted almond, blueberry, raspberry, and coconut.

How many calories are in a glazed donut from Dunkin?

A classic glazed donut from Dunkin has 260 calories, 31 grams of carbs, and 14 grams of fat.

What is the healthiest breakfast item at Dunkin?

The Egg and Cheese Wake Up Wrap is one of the healthiest options with only 180 calories and 9 grams of protein.

Are Dunkin refreshers low calorie?

Dunkin Refreshers contain about 80 to 120 calories for a medium size depending on the flavor you choose.

How can I reduce calories in my Dunkin order?

Switch to almond milk, skip flavor swirls, choose sugar free options, and avoid whipped cream. These small changes can cut your calories in half.
